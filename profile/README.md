# UniView

**See how engineers actually work — before you hire them.**

UniView is the unified interview view. One ~150-minute assessment that
replaces the scattered first-round screens engineers face today — and gives
hiring teams cited evidence per skill, calibrated against real production
work, not algorithmic puzzles.

## What we're building

- **Real engineering scenarios.** Debug a real codebase. Review a real PR.
  Refactor real code. Design and defend a system. No invert-a-binary-tree.
- **Per-skill detected seniority.** We score each skill in a candidate's stack
  separately, with confidence levels and the evidence that produced each call.
- **Honest scope.** We name what we measured *and* what we didn't. "Below
  threshold" stays "Below threshold." We never inflate signal to keep a deal
  moving.
- **Built for both sides.** Engineers take the assessment once and share the
  result with as many hiring teams as they want. Hiring teams unlock the
  candidates worth their engineers' time.

## Who it's for

- **Engineers** — you take it once, you control distribution, it's free
  forever, and your profile carries cited evidence instead of a vibe.
- **Hiring teams** — you replace one early interview round with something that
  actually shows how a candidate works, calibrated against the seniority bar
  you care about.

## What we believe

- The strongest signal of an engineer comes from how they make decisions in
  real engineering work, not how fast they recall an algorithm.
- Companies that want a complete picture of an engineer should treat any
  single assessment as one input among several — never the deciding factor.
- Honest scoring beats inflated scoring. We say what we measured and what we
  didn't.
